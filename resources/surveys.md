# Survey Papers and Resources

## Comprehensive Surveys

### 1. Evolutionary Computation and Large Language Models: A Survey (2025)
- **arXiv:** [2505.15741](https://arxiv.org/abs/2505.15741)
- **Scope:** Broad survey covering both directions of LLM+EA integration
- **Year:** 2025

### 2. Evolutionary Computation in the Era of Large Language Models: Survey and Roadmap (2024)
- **arXiv:** [2401.10034](https://arxiv.org/abs/2401.10034)
- **Scope:** Covers EC enhanced by LLMs and LLMs enhanced by EC
- **Year:** 2024

### 3. A Systematic Survey on Large Language Models for Evolutionary Optimization (2025)
- **arXiv:** [2509.08269](https://arxiv.org/abs/2509.08269)
- **Scope:** Systematic review of LLMs in evolutionary optimization
- **Year:** 2025

### 4. When Large Language Models Meet Evolutionary Algorithms (2024)
- **DOI:** [10.34133/research.0646](https://spj.science.org/doi/10.34133/research.0646)
- **Journal:** Science Partner Journals - Research
- **Scope:** Overview of the intersection of LLMs and EAs
- **Year:** 2024

### 5. Large Language Models as Evolutionary Optimizers (2024)
- **arXiv:** [2310.19046](https://arxiv.org/abs/2310.19046)
- **Scope:** Focused on LLMs as optimization tools
- **Year:** 2024

### 6. A Survey on LLM-based Automated Heuristic Design (2024)
- **Scope:** Focused specifically on heuristic generation
- **Year:** 2024

## Foundational Papers

These papers established the core concepts:

| Paper | Year | Key Contribution |
|---|---|---|
| [ELM](https://arxiv.org/abs/2206.08896) | 2022 | First formal proposal of LLMs as mutation operators |
| [FunSearch](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41586-023-06924-6) | 2023 | First mathematical discovery via LLM+evolution |
| [OPRO](https://arxiv.org/abs/2309.03409) | 2023 | LLMs as general-purpose optimizers |
| [Language Model Crossover](https://arxiv.org/abs/2302.12170) | 2023 | Formalized LLM as crossover operator |
| [AlphaEvolve](https://arxiv.org/abs/2506.13131) | 2025 | Full-scale production impact |
